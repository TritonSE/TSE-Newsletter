EXECUTIVE BOARD UPDATES

01. TSE Google Calendars

We have separated the TSE calendar into 3 calendars to help reduce clutter on your calendars. Please add the following calendars to your personal calendar by clicking on these links (Public, Events, Meetings)! Click the Add Calendar button at the bottom-right corner to add each calendar to yours. If you would like to access this on your personal Google calendar, reach out to Anoushka Dave on Slack.

Triton Software Engineering (Public) - MANDATORY. This calendar contains all of the TSE events (workshops/panels) that are open to all of UC San Diego.
Triton Software Engineering (Events) - MANDATORY. This calendar contains all of the TSE events (closed workshops/panels/social events/all hands/onboarding etc.) solely for TSE members.
Triton Software Engineering (Meetings) - OPTIONAL. This calendar contains all of the TSE-related meetings. Your PMs will be using this to schedule your meetings, and you will be invited to those events. So, adding this calendar is not required; however, you can add it if you are interested in what the rest of the org is up to!

“Don't be afraid to be ambitious. You can do the same things as anyone else. Don't look at someone, for example a CEO, and assume you cannot achieve that same position.

Always keep learning and improving yourself, be curious about everything and keep learning new skills and acquiring new experiences. Take feedback constructively and internalize it to be the best version of yourself as you can be.

Don't be afraid to ask for mentorship and guidance, but make a solid effort before asking.

If you commit to something, do it well and do it right. You get out of it what you put in.”
